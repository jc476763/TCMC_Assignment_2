Goals

To redesign the TCMC website to

  • Recruit new members
  • Increase the involvement of members in the client's activities like volunteering for administration, organising and promoting concerts and workshops
  • Increase ticket sales to the general public
  • encourage performers to let the client organize a concert for them
  • encourage performers to cooperate with promotion, photo shoots, media releases, etc.

Success Evaluation

  • The total number of visitors during a certain period of time.
  • The Difference in the number of registration of new members.
  • Number of concerts organized.
  • The active members in the client's activities.
  • Ticket sales for the concerts and events.
  • concerts organized, promotion, photo shoots, media releases about the event that has been organized.

Target Audience

  • We provide platform for a wide varieties of musical genres and styles which will include people with different age group having different taste in music.
  • The typical audience having specialized taste in musical genres like classical, jazz, ethnic music, etc.
  • Children's music about their culture, other cultures, good behavior, facts and skills and folk songs for entertaining children and their parents

Content

  • Home: Introduction to TCMC and general information about Townsville
  • Events: List of events with its description and images that has already been held along with the date, time and venue of new events upcoming in future that is sorted according to date
  • Artists: Information about the registered artists with TCMC, their biography.
  • Bulletin Board: It contains the information about the opportunities for volunteering, music teachers, invitation for the new bands and band members.
  • Members: This page allows users to register to TCMC by simply downloading the member application form and submit it to the given email address along with the payment receipt that is beig paid using paypal.
  • News Feed: This page contains information about music, artists, events which allows users looking for a music related things and feed them back into the site.
  • About Us: Information and pictures describing who is TCMC, how it is formed, what it does. Also, it contains information about the TCMC sponsors.
  • Contact Us: This page contains contact details such as street address, telephone number, fax, email address and feedback page so that users can send their feedback regarding their experience, compliments and complains to TCMC.
